I am a senior student at Bilkent University Computer Science Department. I would like to continue my research with Ph.D. degree.

My research interests lie broadly in computer vision and machine learning, including image/video processing, multimodality, emotion analysis, attention mechanisms, and image generation with generative adversarial networks. Besides, I am also interested in 3D objects, robotics, natural language processing, and autonomous systems.

Research Projects

Emotion Manipulated Video Generation

3DUniversum

• Research on visual emotion manipulation in the videos.

• Extracted emotion coefficients from 3D reconstructed faces is used to benefit from their control power of the emotion.

• Generative adversarial networks are used to both manipulate emotion and turning into manipulated 3D reconstructed faces to original frames.

• I am currently trying to make lips-sync by adding audio information to the GAN pipeline to enhance consistency between frames.

• We are aiming to submit paper to the ICCV 2021 conference.
